AI Privacy: Understanding User Awareness and Security Risks

A personal research project exploring how AI tools handle user data, what privacy and security risks exist, and whether everyday users truly understand how their information may be stored or used.

What this site covers

  • How AI tools use and store user inputs
  • Real-world AI privacy and security incidents
  • Survey results measuring user awareness
  • Practical and ethical guidelines for safer AI use

Research Question

Are we aware of AI security? How is our personal data at risk when we use AI tools, and how can we use them more safely and ethically?

About

An overview of the purpose behind this project, my motivation for researching AI privacy, and how this work connects to my academic background in Computer Science and ITI.

Research

A research-based overview explaining how AI systems collect, process, and store user data, along with key privacy and security risks associated with modern AI tools.

Results

An analysis of survey data examining how frequently people use AI tools and how aware they are of data privacy and security risks, supported by charts and summarized findings.

Guidelines

Practical recommendations and ethical guidelines for using AI tools responsibly, including safer data-sharing practices and increased awareness of potential security risks.

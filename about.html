About This Project

I’m a Rutgers student double-majoring in Computer Science and ITI. I created this project to study AI privacy and user awareness—how people use AI tools and whether they understand the security risks.

Project Goal

Combine real-world examples, survey data, and practical guidance to help users use AI more safely and ethically. This site is designed as a research showcase and a learning resource.
